My Top 5 Favourite Games

Hey, this is a simple list of 5 games that I think deserve the "best" title.

  • Elden Ring

    The GOAT of all Soulslike games. Amazing enviornments, bosses, exploration, simply a 10 out of 10 in all areas.

  • Minecraft

    Everyone's childhood love. No matter how old I get, or how repetitive the game becomes at times, nostalgia keeps coming me back, especially if I've got my friends playing it too.

  • Bioshock

    The setup, the atmosphere, the truths, the lies, the world design and much more make me want to forget it all and experience it again. Simply a masterpiece.

  • DOOM

    Ngl, if demons killed my pet I'd go through hell and back to exact my revenge as well.

  • IGI

    The one that started it all for me. Nothing beats your first one.
